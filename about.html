About

This calculator can be used to find the greenhouse emissions associated with custom meals and diet plans. By constructing recipes of interest and comparing the emissions, you can find the most environmentally friendly food choices, and compare the footprints of various diets such as no red meat, vegetarian, vegan etc.

e.g. vegetarian recipes may include a higher dairy content than omnivore recipes. However since dairy products such as cheese have a higher emissions footprint per kg than fish and many meats, a vegetarian diet may be no more beneficial than a diet that includes white meat and fish but omits red meat. This calculator can be used to investigate.

Sources

CO2 per kg data for limited food list provided by the Environmental Working Group Meat Eater's Guide.

Calorie data provided by USDA.
